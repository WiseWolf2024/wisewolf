"You become. It takes a long time."

- Margery Williams Bianco

I sigh big or exhale heavy, I’m not sure which, every time I remember that line from The Velveteen Rabbit. Every time I reach a crux in my journey or feel impatient with myself for not having it all figured out yet, I close my eyes and remember that I am in the life-long process of becoming. And it takes a long time. Hi. I’m Katie. I’m a boy mom, a creative, a mystic and an advocate. I hold light-heartedness and depth in both hands; I appreciate and encompass nuance, duality, curiosity and humor. I view most everything as an adventure and I find joy in discovering beauty in almost anything.

My favorite stories are that of the underdog. I feel most alive when I am lost in a new place or standing in the ocean. I feel most inspired when I am gifted the opportunity to witness the transformation of another. Wherever you are in your ever-unfolding story, I’m honored to walk along beside you as both a witness and a fellow traveler. Spiritual Direction is a sacred space that we create together. Through safe sharing and careful listening, you may experience the gentle untangling of your soul as you softly come home to yourself.
